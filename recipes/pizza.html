This pepperoni pizza recipe produces a quick and easy classc! Delicious homemake pizza crust and tomato sauce have never been easier.

Ingredients

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 (12 ounce) can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oragano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
